## üìå Key Concepts Covered

### 1. Cloud Business Value
- Understand cloud advantages vs. traditional IT  
- Calculate Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) and ROI  
- Identify business benefits from different deployment models  

### 2. Cloud Service Models
- **IaaS**: Infrastructure as a Service  
- **PaaS**: Platform as a Service  
- **SaaS**: Software as a Service  
- When and why to choose each based on business needs  

### 3. Cloud Governance & Compliance
- Learn compliance requirements and risk management  
- Understand privacy and regulatory concerns  
- Map responsibilities between providers and organizations  

### 4. Vendor Contract Management
- Evaluate key financial and operational terms  
- Negotiate contracts to prevent vendor lock-in  
- Understand cost optimization strategies  

### 5. Translating Technical Terms
- Convert virtualization, storage, networking into business language  
- Communicate effectively with technical teams and stakeholders
